Living AI Companions
Converse with digital recreations of real elders — trained on preserved voice recordings. Ask your AI Dada about Shyambazar in 1962, or your AI Dadi about Durga Puja rituals. Every response grounded in actual memory. Powered by Claude claude-sonnet-4-20250514 with Bengali NLP fine-tuning.

AR Time Portal
Point your camera at any Kolkata street and see it as it looked in 1940, 1962, or 1985 — overlaid with geotagged story pins from elders who lived there. Spatial storytelling anchored to real locations.
📍 Geo-anchored · AR WebXR

Voice Studio

🧠 NLP · Whisper ASR

Memory Constellation
3D interactive network — stories, people, places orbit each other like stars. Drag, zoom, explore connections across decades.
🌌 WebGL · 3D Force Graph

Impact Observatory
Live dashboards — stories by neighbourhood, language distribution, family connections, scalability to all Indian cities.
📊 Real-time Analytics

Tagore-Style Generative Narratives
AI composes entirely new stories in the literary styles of Rabindranath Tagore and Satyajit Ray — drawing from preserved memories. Each story is unique, culturally rooted, and emotionally resonant. Your grandfather's anecdote transformed into a Tagore-esque short story.
🖋️ Generative AI · Bengali Literature

Virtual Adda Rooms
Real-time multiplayer digital Adda spaces. Families separated by continents gather to listen to stories together, ask questions of AI companions, share their own memories. The communal spirit of Adda — alive across distance and time.
